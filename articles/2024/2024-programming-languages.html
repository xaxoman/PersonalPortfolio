Which programming language should you study in 2024?

Posted on March 26, 2024

There are a lot of programming languages out there and it can be tough to choose which one you should dedicate your time to. In this article, I'm going to show you which one you should pick from the perspective of a computer science student.

Why should you study a programming language?

  1. Job opportunities: In today's day and age, knowing at least one programming language can help you find more job opportunities regardless of your aspirations, whether you want to be a data analyst, a web developer, or work in finance, marketing, etc...
  2. Smart working: Working from the comfort of your home is a reality for many programmers who are fortunate enough to do so. Since programmers only require their computers for work, they don't have to worry about going to the office. They can work from home and manage their work schedule with more flexibility.
  3. You can work abroad: Programming allows you to work wherever you want. You can fly to another country and still work perfectly. The only thing needed is a laptop and an internet connection.
  4. Build your own business: Learning a new programming language can be beneficial if you are looking to start your own business, or if you already have one and want to improve it. With programming knowledge, you can create your own website, collect data from your clients, and perform other essential tasks that can help your business grow and succeed.

Which programming language should I study?

It all depends on what your goal is. For example, if you want to be a web developer you need to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, but if your goal is to become a data analyst then it's preferred to learn languages like Python, SQL, and R lang. Find why you want to learn how to program in the first place, then investigate which programming language is most commonly used in your field.

Where do I start learning once I pick one?

There are a lot of places to start learning, for example:

  • FreeCodeCamp
  • CodeAcademy
  • W3schools
  • The Odin Project (for web development)
  • Envato Plus

Isn't AI going to replace programmers?

It's important to understand that while AI is an excellent tool for programmers, it cannot fully replace them. However, it can drastically enhance their productivity and speed, making their work more efficient and effective.

Conclusion

In conclusion, learning a programming language can open up a world of opportunities for you and your career. Whether you want to work in tech, build your own business, or just have a valuable skill, programming is a great option. It's important to choose the right programming language based on your goals and aspirations, and then find the best resources to start learning. With hard work, dedication, and the right tools, you can become a skilled programmer and take advantage of all the benefits that come with it.
